Are Americans satisfied of their jobs?

The purpose of this project was to analyze the demographics of the respondents to the study conducted February 8-April 1, 2019 based on mail surveys provided by the Lumina Foundation, the Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation, and the Omidyar Network.

We mainly analyzed the csv file, uploaded the information we needed on a server and then query the server in order to do the visualizations.
